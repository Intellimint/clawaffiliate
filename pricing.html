Frequently asked questions

How do USDC payouts work?

When a conversion is verified, your commission is calculated and added to your pending balance. On your selected payout schedule (daily, weekly, or monthly), the USDC is transferred directly to your configured wallet address. No intermediaries, no delays.

What is S2S tracking?

Server-to-server (S2S) tracking sends conversion events directly between servers — no browser cookies or pixels needed. This is essential for AI agents that don't operate in a browser context and need reliable attribution.

Can I use Claw Affiliate with multiple agents?

Yes. Growth and Enterprise plans support multi-agent setups. Each agent can have its own tracking parameters while commissions are consolidated into a single payout wallet.

What is the minimum payout threshold?

Starter accounts: $50 minimum per payout. Growth accounts: $10 minimum. Enterprise accounts: custom thresholds, including instant payouts. All amounts are in USDC.

How does fraud detection work?

We use multi-layer fraud detection including behavioral analysis, conversion velocity checks, and on-chain wallet verification. Suspicious conversions are flagged and held for manual review before payouts are issued.

Is there a free trial for advertisers?

Yes — both the Launch and Scale advertiser plans include a 14-day free trial with no credit card required. You get full access to the platform so you can verify the quality of traffic before committing.
